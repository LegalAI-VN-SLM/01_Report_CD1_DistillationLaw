\section{Goal of this topic}

The goal of this project is to develop an AI-powered chatbot that provides trustworthy, personalized, and up-to-date university admission advice for students in Vietnam. By integrating information from multiple sources, such as official university websites, government portals, and online communities, the system aims to reduce misinformation, minimize the time students spend searching for accurate details, and improve the overall efficiency of the admission consultation process.
Beyond delivering answers, the chatbot will be designed with enhanced reasoning capabilities and Explainable AI (XAI) techniques. This allows it not only to respond accurately but also to explain the rationale behind its suggestions. Such transparency helps students understand the logic used in each recommendation, builds trust, and supports more informed and confident academic decision-making. Ultimately, the system seeks to improve accessibility to reliable guidance, reduce information overload, and promote fairness and clarity in the admission process.
