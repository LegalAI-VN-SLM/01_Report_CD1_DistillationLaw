Formulas (evidence)

Cong thuc duoi day la nen tang de dien giai cac chi so trong bao cao. Moi cong thuc di kem y nghia ngan gon.

Cross-entropy loss
$$L = -\frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^{N} \log p(y_i \mid x_i)$$

Do mat mat trung binh tren tap train/eval; giam thi tot hon.

Perplexity
$$\mathrm{PPL} = \exp(L)$$

PPL la exp cua loss; PPL thap hon nghia la mo hinh tu tin hon.

Accuracy
$$\mathrm{Acc} = \frac{\#\mathrm{correct}}{N}$$

Dung cho MC/NLI; can tach "unknown" de danh gia chinh xac hon.

F1 score
$$\mathrm{F1} = \frac{2PR}{P + R}$$

Can bang giua precision va recall; nhay cam voi bias nhan.

ROUGE-L (F1)
$$\mathrm{ROUGE\text{-}L} = \frac{(1 + \beta^2)\, \mathrm{LCS}}{\mathrm{len}(ref) + \beta^2 \, \mathrm{len}(hyp)}$$

Do do tuong dong subsequence dai nhat giua output va reference.
